About

Many students spend money daily without knowing where it goes. Student Finance Tracker helps students record expenses, monitor budgets, and make informed financial decisions, right in your browser.

Why Use This App?

  • Know where your money goes

    Every transaction is logged so you always have a clear picture of your spending.

  • Stay within your budget

    Set a monthly budget cap and get instant alerts before you overspend.

  • Build better spending habits

    Visualise 7-day trends and category breakdowns to spot patterns early.

  • Backup your financial records

    Export your data as JSON anytime and import it back with one click.
